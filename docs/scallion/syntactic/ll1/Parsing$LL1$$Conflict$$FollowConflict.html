case class FollowConflict(source: Disjunction[_], root: Sequence[_, _], ambiguities: Set[(Parsing.this)#Kind]) extends Conflict with Product with Serializable

Indicates that an ambiguity arises due to a disjunction appearing somewhere in the left-hand side of a sequence, that conflicts with the right-hand side of that sequence.
